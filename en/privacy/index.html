Privacy Policy

Privacy policy and cookie notice for this website.

Jun 24, 2026 3 minutes read
简体中文
语速

This privacy policy explains how svtter.cn (“we”, “this site”) collects, uses, and protects data when you visit. We comply with applicable data protection regulations.

Data We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit this site, the server and third-party services may automatically record the following non-personal information:

  • Access logs (IP address, access time, requested URL, browser User-Agent, referring page)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level, inferred from IP)
  • Aggregated statistics such as device and browser type, screen resolution, etc.

This information is used only for operations, security auditing, and traffic analytics. It is not used to identify you personally.

Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you contact this site via email, comments, or forms, the name, email, and message you provide are stored solely to respond to you or handle your request.

Use of Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. This site uses cookies to operate properly and to improve your experience.

Essential Cookies

Cookies required for basic site functionality (sessions, security tokens) cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

This site uses Google Analytics (GA4) to analyze traffic. Google Analytics collects anonymous visit data (page views, time on site, traffic source, etc.) via cookies.

  • You can disable GA data collection by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • See Google’s privacy policy and terms for details.

Third-Party Advertising and Google AdSense

This site displays ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to measure ad performance and serve more relevant ads.
  • You can manage personalized ad preferences via Google Ads Settings, or disable personalized advertising from third-party vendors at aboutads.info.

If your browser is set to reject cookies, affected features (including ads) may not work properly.

Embedded Third-Party Content

Some articles on this site may embed content from third-party websites (videos, images, demos). These third parties may collect data according to their own privacy policies when you interact with their content.

How We Protect Data

This site transmits data over HTTPS and restricts access to logs and backend data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Data Retention

  • Server access logs are retained as needed for operations and deleted automatically after expiry.
  • Information you submit voluntarily is kept only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was provided.

Your Rights

To the extent permitted by applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Know what data this site holds about you;
  • Request correction or deletion of your data;
  • Withdraw consent for non-essential cookies (via browser settings or the ad settings links above).

Children’s Privacy

This site is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe data has been collected in error, please contact us using the details below and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, with the date above indicating the latest revision. Continued use of this site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact:

  • Email: blog@svtter.cn
  • Site owner: svtter
