Poor Performance of Large Models on Specific Tasks

Poor performance of large models on specific tasks: Differences from visual recognition to formatted text processing

Jun 19, 2025 1 minute read
简体中文
语速

Vision large models perform poorly on some specific tasks but perform better with formatted text. Here, I use the localization of meter reading areas as an example to demonstrate the performance of large models.

Test Tasks

  2. Extract the meter reading area from the image.

Analysis Summary

From these test results, we can observe:

  1. Differences in Visual Recognition Capabilities: Different models exhibit significant performance variations when handling the same visual task.
  2. Formatted Text Processing: Compared to visual tasks, models perform more stably when processing structured text.
  3. Model Characteristics: Each model has its unique strengths and limitations.

These results remind us to evaluate the suitability of AI models based on specific task types when making selections.
